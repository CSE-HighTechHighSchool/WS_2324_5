Our Submarines

  • Design Safety Interior Speed

Design

Our submarines are meticulously engineered underwater vessels with hydrodynamic hulls designed to minimize water resistance, ensuring efficient underwater navigation. It incorporates a variety of specialized compartments, such as ballast tanks for buoyancy control and advanced sonar systems for navigation and communication. Our submarines contain dual-purpose conning towers, which provide both periscope access for above-water observation and airtight protection for the crew during submerged trips.

Safety

Our submarines prioritize crew safety with pressure hulls and airtight compartments, allowing them to withstand extreme underwater pressures and ensuring the well-being of the personnel within. Extensive safety protocols and rigorous training are essential for submarine operations, making sure that the crew is well-prepared to respond to any emergencies, such as flooding, fires, or equipment failures. Submarines are equipped with redundant systems and emergency escape procedures, including escape hatches, to provide crew members with means of evacuation in case of any dire situations.

Interior

The interior of our submarines are meticulously organized and compact, where every inch of space is optimized for functionality. Crew compartments are designed to accommodate sleeping quarters, workstations, and communal spaces while maximizing comfort and efficiency. Advanced technology and equipment are seamlessly integrated, enabling crew members to operate and maintain complex systems within the confined, and highly specialized, interior of the submarine.

Speed

Our submarines are renowned for their remarkable underwater speed, often reaching impressive speeds that can exceed 25 knots (29 miles per hour) or more, due to the powerful propulsion systems. The ability to cruise at high speeds beneath the ocean's surface makes our submarines a the best on the market for underwater trips and luxorious living.
