The Company

Our company has the main goal of providing the safest and most luxorious submarine trips for all of our passengers to enjoy. We show our paying passengers many underwater history landmarks or any of the beautiful scenery that they want to see. In recent years, we have made great advancements in safety, speed, and overall enjoyment to provide the best service possible.

Company's History

2003

Ocean's Expanse was founded by the three owners, Thomas Sherlock, James Chen, and Bhargav Yerramsetty, with the idea to provide people with the chance to be on luxorious submarine trips underwater.

2006

The first Mark I submarine, M1, was created and remotely tested by the owners. The test failed after reaching a depth of 1000 ft, 304.8 m, but it provided necessary information in order to go further.

2008

The first human piloted submarine, Mark II, was created. It was very large, slow, and uncomfortable, but was sucessfully reached a depth of 5000 ft, 1524 m, and returned to the surface.

2014

The Mark III submarine was created which improved upon the Mark II's speed and fuel efficiency.

2018

This year, both the Mark IV and Mark V were created. Mark IV focused more on decreasing the size of the submarine, while Mark V focused more on reaching deeper depths.

2021

Mark VI was created which improved upon every aspect of the past iterations. It was able to move at double the speeds, was more luxorious, had more space for the passengers while being smaller overall, and was able to reach a depth of 20000 ft, or 6096 m.

2023

Currently, we at Ocean's Expanse has made the most technologically advanced and comfortable sumbarine. It can reach depths up to 35000 ft, 10668 m, and can hold up to 20 passengers when filled. It is the safest submarine to exist and is almost indestructible. We look forward to providing even better submarines in the near future.
